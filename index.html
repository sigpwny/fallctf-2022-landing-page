Fall CTF 2022

What

Fall CTF is a 6 hour hacking event for UIUC students hosted by SIGPwny. The event is open to all students of all majors and backgrounds. Beginners welcome! There will be a series of cybersecurity related challenges including web hacking, reverse engineering, binary exploitation, and cryptography. Join with a friend for teams of up to 2 people. There will be food, companies, and prizes. Come learn and have fun with us! Register for free pizza and a free shirt at our form!

When/Where

September 24th 2022 12 - 6PM Siebel Center for Computer Science in room 1404
